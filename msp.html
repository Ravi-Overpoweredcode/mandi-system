‚ö†Ô∏è Important Information Related to MSP

  • MSP is declared by the Central Government for selected crops based on recommendations of the Commission for Agricultural Costs and Prices (CACP).
  • Sugarcane is NOT covered under MSP. It is governed by a separate pricing mechanism called FRP (Fair and Remunerative Price).
  • MSP is announced at the national level, but actual procurement at MSP depends on state government agencies and procurement infrastructure.
  • Procurement at MSP is not guaranteed for all crops in all states. Wheat and paddy are procured widely, while pulses and oilseeds may have limited procurement.
  • MSP is advisory in nature and does not provide a legal guarantee to purchase crops at MSP.
  • Farmers are advised to verify MSP-related announcements through official government notifications.
